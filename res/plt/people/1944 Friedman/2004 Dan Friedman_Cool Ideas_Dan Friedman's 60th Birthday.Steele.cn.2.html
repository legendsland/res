Summary

Guy Steele 就 Dan Friedman 发表了主题演讲，重点介绍了 Friedman 对 Scheme 的影响深远的思想。Steele 讲述了他们数十年的合作，重点讨论了 Scheme 中的 cons 单元、暂停和自杀式暂停等关键概念，以及这些最初在印第安纳大学开发的想法如何影响了 Scheme 的设计。他还讨论了 Friedman 在非确定性数据结构（如叶状结构）和并行编程（连接机器列表）方面的工作。Steele 还谈到了程序化教学的历史，引用了 BF Skinner 和 Don Parker，以及这些思想如何与 Friedman 的工作相交叉。演讲以庆祝 Friedman 60 岁生日和他对计算机科学的贡献结束。

Guy Steele gives a keynote about Dan Friedman, highlighting Friedman’s influential ideas on Scheme. Steele recounts their decades-long collaboration, focusing on key concepts like cons cells, suspensions, and suicidal suspensions in Scheme, and how these ideas, initially developed at Indiana University, influenced Scheme’s design. He also discusses Friedman’s work on non-deterministic data structures (like fronds) and parallel programming (connection machine list). Steele also touches on the history of programmed instruction, referencing B.F. Skinner and Don Parker, and how these ideas intersected with Friedman’s work. The talk concludes with a celebration of Friedman’s 60th birthday and his contributions to computer science.
