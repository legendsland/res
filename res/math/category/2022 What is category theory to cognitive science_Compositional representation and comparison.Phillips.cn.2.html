范畴论学家和认知科学家研究感兴趣的领域之间的结构（类比）关系，尽管它们处于不同的背景中，即形式系统和心理系统。尽管有这种基本的共同点，但很少有认知科学家采用范畴论方法来理解认知结构，这就提出了一个问题：范畴论对认知科学意味着什么？答案是“范畴论之于认知科学，就像函子之于表示；就像自然变换之于比较”，以使范畴论更易于认知科学家理解和参考。

Category theorists and cognitive scientists study the structural (analogical) relations between domains of interest albeit in different contexts, that is, formal and psychological systems, respectively. Despite this basic commonality, very few cognitive scientists take a category theory approach toward understanding the structure of cognition which raises the question, What is category theory to cognitive science? An answer is given as the slogan “Category theory is to cognitive science as functor is to representation; as natural transformation is to comparison” to make category theory more accessible and informative for cognitive scientists.

关键词

范畴论，范畴，函子，自然变换，类比，矩阵推理

KEYWORDS

category theory, category, functor, natural transformation, analogy, matrix reasoning

1. 简介

1. Introduction

范畴论与认知科学有何关系？简而言之，尽管语境不同，但这两个领域都是关于“（组合）结构的比较”的。范畴论的发明是为了形式化数学构造之间的对应关系（Eilenberg 和 Mac Lane，1945 年；Mac Lane，1998 年）。认知科学家通常从表示的角度看待认知，这种表示通过对应心理状态之间的关系来保留实体关系（结构）：例如，经典的组合性（Fodor 和 Pylyshyn，1988 年）。尽管语境不同，但范畴论的思想与认知科学中的概念相关，而认知科学家通常没有认识到这一点。这里总结了数学结构理论（即范畴论）与认知结构之间的一些基本联系，目的是使范畴论更容易被认知科学家理解和参考。

What is category theory to cognitive science? A short answer is that both fields are about “comparison of (compositional) structure” albeit in different contexts. Category theory was invented to formalize correspondences between mathematical constructions (Eilenberg and Mac Lane, 1945; Mac Lane, 1998). Cognitive scientists often view cognition in terms of representations that preserve entity relationships (structure) via relationships between corresponding mental states: e.g., classical compositionality (Fodor and Pylyshyn, 1988). Despite contextual differences, category theory ideas relate to concepts in cognitive science in ways not generally recognized by cognitive scientists. Some basic connections between a mathematical theory of structure (i.e., category theory) and the structure of cognition are educed here for the purpose of making category theory more accessible and informative to cognitive scientists.

可以说，范畴论和认知科学有着共同的理想：表示和比较某个感兴趣领域中的（组合）结构实体。这种情况可以用下面的箭头方块来表示，表示表达式“约翰爱玛丽”（图 1）。短语和概念之间的关系被描述为通过沿水平箭头滑动将表示表达式结构的左边垂直箭头传输或转换为表示概念结构的右边垂直箭头，从而形成一个方块。这种排列构成了所谓的交换方块，因为逆时针方向的箭头链等于顺时针方向的箭头链，即 ρ◦loves = loves◦ρ，其中◦表示将箭头组合成箭头的操作。这种安排让人联想到语言学中将句法与语义联系起来的组合性原则（例如，参见Janssen，1997；Coecke 等人，2010 ），或认知心理学中类比的结构映射理论（Gentner，1983），类比是从知识源领域到知识目标领域的映射，它出现在各种类比模型（Gentner and Forbus，2011）和隐喻模型（Fuyama 等人，2020）中。

One might say that category theory and cognitive science share a common ideal: the representation and comparison of (compositionally) structured entities in some domain of interest. This situation is illustrated as the following square of arrows for the expression, John loves Mary (Figure 1). The relationship between the phrase and the concept is depicted as transporting or transforming the left vertical arrow, representing the structure of the expression, to the right vertical arrow, representing the conceptual structure, by sliding along the horizontal arrows, thus forming a square. This arrangement constitutes a so-called commutative square in that the chain of arrows in the anticlockwise direction equals the chain of arrows in the clockwise direction, that is, ρ ◦ loves = loves ◦ ρ, where ◦ signifies the operation for combining arrows to form arrows. This arrangement is reminiscent of the compositionality principle (see, e.g., Janssen, 1997; Coecke et al., 2010) in linguistics linking syntax to semantics, or the structure mapping theory (Gentner, 1983) of analogy in cognitive psychology as a map from a source domain of knowledge to a target domain of knowledge, which features in a variety of analogy models (Gentner and Forbus, 2011) and a model of metaphor (Fuyama et al., 2020).

尽管范畴论具有抽象性质，但该理论的很大一部分内容都是围绕（类型化）交换平方的概念展开的。在认知的背景下，基本范畴论构造对应于组合表征和比较或转换，这与表征/计算心理理论（Wilson，1999）相一致，其中认知被视为认知表征上的计算过程系统（有关此观点和其他观点的综述，请参阅Fodor，1985）。本文的目的是使这种一致性对认知科学家来说更加具体。本介绍的其余部分是为后面的细节做准备的预览。

Despite the abstract nature of category theory, a substantial amount of the theory organizes around this notion of (typed) commutative square. In the context of cognition, basic category theory constructions correspond to compositional representation and comparison or transformation, which comports with the representational/computational theory of mind (Wilson, 1999) whereby cognition is seen as a system of computational processes over cognitive representations (see, Fodor, 1985, for a survey of this and other views). The purpose of this article is to make this consilience more concrete for cognitive scientists. The rest of this introduction is a preview to prime the details that follow.

范畴论将这个将结构保存为交换平方的简单想法极大地形式化了，其应用远远超出了数学的范围（例如，参见Fong 和 Spivak, 2019）。近年来，对范畴论的跨学科应用有着共同兴趣的研究人员聚集在一起，形成了应用范畴论这个领域。从历史上看，这个箭头的“平方”以多种形式出现，最早是自然变换的形式概念（Eilenberg and Mac Lane, 1945 ），它依赖于函子和范畴的概念。这些概念之间的关系用箭头图来描绘（图 2A）。范畴由对象和对象之间的（有向）关系组成，称为箭头：例如，f是从对象A到对象B 的箭头，在某些范畴C中也写为f : A → B。虚线表示两个函子对C中的对象和箭头的作用，即函子F将A、B和f发送给某个类别D中的对象F ( A ) 和F ( B ) 以及箭头F ( f ) ，函子G将A、B和f发送给同样在D中的对象G ( A ) 和G ( B ) 以及箭头G ( f ) 。函子是类别对象之间的箭头：例如，F, G : C → D。对象F ( A ) 和F ( B ) 以及箭头F ( f ) 构成F的像；同样，对象G ( A ) 和G ( B ) 以及箭头G ( f ) 构成G的像。涉及F和G像的箭头的（自然性）正方形描绘了自然变换，即从F到G的映射η ，写为，由对C中每个对象A 的分量映射η A : F ( A ) → F ( A ) 组成，并且对于C中的每个箭头f ，平方都是可交换的。因此，自然变换是函子之间的映射，函子是范畴之间的映射，因此存在逻辑依赖关系。尽管范畴论构造最初是为了拓扑学应用而引入的（Eilenberg and Mac Lane，1945 年），但它也出现在各种领域，例如自然变换在音乐中的应用（Mannone and Favali，2019 年）和美学中组合性的范畴形式（Kubota 等人，2017 年）。

Category theory vastly formalizes this simple idea of preserving structure as commutative squares with applications well beyond mathematics (see, e.g., Fong and Spivak, 2019). In recent years, researchers with a common interest in interdisciplinary applications of category theory have coalesced as the field known as Applied Category Theory. This “square” of arrows appears in many guises, historically, beginning with the formal concept of natural transformation (Eilenberg and Mac Lane, 1945), which depends on the concepts of functor and category. The relationships between these concepts are depicted as a diagram of arrows (Figure 2A). A category consists of objects and (directed) relations between objects, called arrows: e.g., f is an arrow from an object A to an object B, also written f:A → B, in some category C. The dotted lines indicate the actions of two functors on the objects and arrows in C, that is, respectively, a functor F sends A, B, and f to objects F(A) and F(B) and arrow F(f) in some category D and a functor G sends A, B, and f to the objects G(A) and G(B) and arrow G(f) also in D. Functors are arrows between objects that are categories: e.g., F, G:C → D. The objects F(A) and F(B) and the arrow F(f) constitute the image of F; likewise, the objects G(A) and G(B) and the arrow G(f) constitute the image of G. The (naturality) square of arrows involving the images of F and G depicts a natural transformation, that is, a map η from F to G, written , consisting of a component map ηA:F(A) → F(A) for each object A in C such that for each arrow f in C the square commutes. Thus, natural transformations are maps between functors and functors are maps between categories, hence the logical dependencies. Although originally introduced for applications in topology (Eilenberg and Mac Lane, 1945), category theory constructions also feature in diverse fields such as the use of natural transformations in music (Mannone and Favali, 2019) and categorical forms of compositionality in aesthetics (Kubota et al., 2017).

范畴、函子和自然变换属于广义理论的另一个重要方面——组合性：两个兼容箭头（即一对“首尾相连”的箭头）的组合是一个箭头，例如f : A → B与g : B → C组合为g ◦ f : A → C，其中 ◦ 再次表示组合操作。组合在所有层面上进行：对象之间的箭头、范畴之间的函子以及函子之间的自然变换。箭头图（图 2B）涉及箭头的组合，f : A → B和g : B → C，函子对组合箭头的作用，F ( g ◦ f )，以及自然变换的组合，和。 （该图未显示函子组合，即在第三个平面外方向。）这些看似不同的组合形式实际上是不同上下文（或维度）中的相同概念：普通箭头是普通对象之间的箭头（垂直维度），函子是类别对象之间的箭头，自然变换是函子类别中函子对象之间的箭头（水平维度）。​​自然变换也与函子组合。范畴论提供了与认知科学相关的组合性概念的广泛概括。

Categories, functors, and natural transformations partake in another important aspect of the general theory—compositionality: the composition of two compatible arrows (i.e., a pair of arrows linked “head to tail”) is an arrow, for example, f:A → B composed with g:B → C is g ◦ f:A → C, where again ◦ signifies the composition operation. Composition operates at all levels: arrows between objects, functors between categories and natural transformations between functors. The diagram of arrows (Figure 2B) involves composition of arrows, f:A → B and g:B → C, the action of functors on composed arrows, F(g ◦ f), and composition of natural transformations, and . (The diagram does not show functor composition, that is, in the third, out-of-plane direction.) These apparently different forms of compositionality are actually the same concept in different contexts (or dimensions): ordinary arrows are arrows between ordinary objects (vertical dimension), functors are arrows between objects that are categories, and natural transformations are arrows between objects that are functors in a category of functors (horizontal dimension). Natural transformations also compose with functors. Category theory provides a vast generalization of the notion of compositionality that is relevant to cognitive science.

尽管以这种方式直截了当地介绍了基本范畴论概念，但由于只字未提其具体性质，因此在应用方面几乎无法引发直觉。然而，对于认知科学家来说，这些对象之间关系的平方也让人联想到矩阵推理任务（Raven 等人，1998）中使用的刺激平方，矩阵推理任务已作为智力测试进行研究（Carpenter 等人，1990）。此类推理任务涉及一个缺少单元格的刺激矩阵，可以通过应用从所有单元格都填满的行或列中得出的关系来完成。例如，矩阵形式中的比例类比“母马与小马驹的关系相当于母牛与什么的关系？”涉及一个二乘二的矩阵。通过推断母马和小马驹之间的相关关系是生育，并将此关系应用于母牛以获得小牛，空单元格用小牛填充。这些和其他类似的刺激矩阵具有一个共同的形式，用表达式a : b :: c : d表示，其中分号对应于一个（例如，垂直）方向的关系，双分号对应于另一个（水平）方向的关系。这种情况类似于交换平方（图 3），它自然扩展到具有更多行和列的矩阵（参见图 2B）。这样的比较提供了一种清晰的方法来引导关于范畴论概念的直觉（第 2 节），以便在认知科学中的潜在应用（第 3 节）来补充其他概念化（例如，参见Phillips，2021a及其中的参考文献）。

Basic category theory concepts, though straightforwardly introduced this way, engender little intuition regarding applications as nothing is said about their specific nature. For cognitive scientists, however, these squares of relations between objects are also reminiscent of the squares of stimuli used in matrix reasoning tasks (Raven et al., 1998), which have been studied as tests of intelligence (Carpenter et al., 1990). Such reasoning tasks involve a matrix of stimuli with a missing cell that can be completed by applying the relationship educed from the rows or columns with all cells filled. For instance, the proportional analogy Mare is to foal as cow is to what? in matrix form involves a two-by-two matrix. The empty cell is filled with calf by educing that the relevant relationship between mare and foal is gives-birth-to and applying this relationship to cow to obtain calf . These and other such matrices of stimuli have a common form symbolized by the expression a:b::c:d, where the semicolon corresponds to relationships in one (say, vertical) direction and the double semicolon to relationships in the other (horizontal) direction. This situation is analogous to a commutative square (Figure 3), which naturally extends to matrices with more rows and columns (cf. Figure 2B). Such comparisons afford a perspicuous way of bootstrapping intuitions about category theory concepts (Section 2) for potential applications in cognitive science (Section 3) to complement other conceptualizations (see, e.g., Phillips, 2021a, and the references therein).

一些读者可能会考虑，鉴于已经存在许多介绍，是否需要详细解释范畴论的基本概念。然而，范畴论的介绍通常假定了一种思维方式，这种思维方式可能会困扰那些寻求直觉的目标受众之外的人（有关普通读者，请参阅Lawvere 和 Schanuel，2009年）。对这些读者来说，该理论似乎毫无意义。然而，可以说，数学既反映了思考，也反映了世界（Mac Lane，1986 年；Lakoff 和 Núñez，2000 年）。这里的目的是向认知科学家介绍范畴论概念，使他们能够分类地思考思考。因此，这种介绍方式摆脱了将技术细节放到附录中的通常风格，而是进行并排比较以便于理解（魔鬼存在于可比较的细节中），这为进行认知科学提供了一种新颖的概念综合。

Some readers may ponder the need for detailed explanations of basic category theory concepts given the many introductions that already exist. However, category theory introductions typically presume a style of thinking that can bedevil those outside the target audience seeking intuition (see Lawvere and Schanuel, 2009, for a general readership). For those readers, the theory can appear as a bridge to nowhere. Yet mathematics is arguably as much a reflection of thinking as it is about the world (Mac Lane, 1986; Lakoff and Núñez, 2000). The purpose here is to present category theory concepts to cognitive scientists in a way that enables thinking about thinking, categorically. Accordingly, this presentation departs from the usual style of relegating technical details to an appendix in favor of a side-by-side comparison to facilitate understanding—the devil is in the comparable details—which affords a novel synthesis of concepts for the purpose of doing cognitive science.

2. 一些形式和概念上的比较

2. Some formal and conceptual comparisons

这里采用的比较的形式基础是自然变换的概念，它依赖于函子的概念，而函子又依赖于范畴的概念。因此，首先介绍范畴（第 2.1 节），然后介绍函子（第 2.2 节），然后介绍自然变换（第 2.3 节）。

The formal basis of comparison employed here is the concept of natural transformation, which depends on the concept of functor, which in turn depends of the concept of category. Accordingly, categories are introduced first (Section 2.1), followed by functors (Section 2.2), and then natural transformations (Section 2.3).

2.1. 类别和组成结构

2.1. Categories and compositional structure

范畴论和认知科学都涉及对某些感兴趣的“领域”的（组合）结构进行建模，即实体、实体关系以及这些关系的组合本身的相关方式。（术语“领域”有两种含义：非正式地，作为感兴趣的主题；正式地，作为箭头的源对象，第 2.1.2 节）。例如，在认知科学中，人们可以将认知过程视为由一系列子过程组成，将认知系统视为由子系统组成，或将某个复杂实体的认知表征视为由组成实体的表征构成的。范畴论还考虑了各种各样的组合形式，包括函数、关系、代数结构和空间的组合。所有形式都基于这里介绍的组合性概念，该概念也适用于认知科学家感兴趣的情况。

Both category theory and cognitive science are concerned with modeling the (compositional) structure of some “domain” of interest, that is, the entities, the entity relationships, and the way that combinations for those relationships are themselves related. (The term domain is used in two senses: informally, as a topic of interest and, formally, as the source object of an arrow, Section 2.1.2). In cognitive science, for example, one may regard a cognitive process as consisting of a chain of subprocesses, a cognitive system as composed of subsystems, or a cognitive representation of some complex entity as constructed from representations of constituent entities. Category theory also considers a wide variety of compositional forms, including composition of functions, relations, algebraic structures, and spaces. All forms are based on a concept of compositionality, introduced here, that also pertains to situations of interest to cognitive scientists.

类别的概念取决于几个组成概念。简而言之，类别由对象和箭头的集合组成（第 2.1.1 节），其中每个箭头从其域对象指向其陪域对象（第 2.1.2 节），包括自导向恒等箭头（第 2.1.3 节），它们通过满足某些属性的组合操作组合成箭头（第 2.1.4 节）。类别的正式定义（第 2.1.5 节）将结构的概念捕捉为箭头：此类结构的集合是此类箭头（现在是对象）的类别，这些对象之间的箭头是结构保持映射，以交换平方给出（第 2.1.6 节）。

The concept of a category depends on several constituent concepts. Briefly, a category consists of a collection of objects and arrows (Section 2.1.1) where each arrow is directed from its domain object to its codomain object (Section 2.1.2), including self-directed identity arrows (Section 2.1.3), that combine as arrows by a composition operation satisfying certain properties (Section 2.1.4). A formal definition of category (Section 2.1.5) captures the notion of structure as an arrow: The collection of such structures is a category of such arrows (now objects), and the arrows between these objects are structure-preserving maps given as commutative squares (Section 2.1.6).

2.1.1. 元素与对象和箭头相关

2.1.1. Elements are related as objects and arrows

通常从元素及其元素间关系的角度来考虑感兴趣的领域（比如一群人）。有序集是如何以这种方式对领域进行建模的简单示例。例如，假设有三个感兴趣的人，Ann、Bob和Cal，并且Ann 比 Bob 矮，而 Bob 比 Cal 矮。这个领域可以建模为一个有序集，即人员集合P = { Ann, Bob, Cal } 以及他们的顺序关系Ann < Bob和Bob < Cal。这些元素及其顺序关系对应于一个类别的两种基本成分的实例，即每个元素对应于实体的抽象概念的一个实例，称为对象，每个顺序关系对应于实体（对象）之间关系的抽象概念的一个实例，称为箭头或态射或映射。因此，例如，元素Ann现在是一个对象，而关系Ann < Bob现在是某个对应于有序集的类别中的箭头Ann → Bob 。

A domain of interest (such as a group of people) is typically considered in terms of its elements and relationships between those elements. An ordered set is a simple example of how one might model a domain this way. Suppose, for instance, three people of interest, Ann, Bob, and Cal, and that Ann is shorter than Bob and Bob is shorter than Cal. This domain can be modeled as an ordered set, that is, the set of people P = {Ann, Bob, Cal} together with their order relationships Ann < Bob and Bob < Cal. These elements and their order relationships correspond to instances of two basic kinds of constituents for a category, that is, each element corresponds to an instance of an abstract notion of entity, called object, and each order relationship to an instance of an abstract notion of relation between entities (objects), called arrow or morphism or map. So, for instance, the element Ann is now an object and the relationship Ann < Bob is now an arrow Ann → Bob in some category corresponding to an ordered set.

元素之于关系，犹如对象之于箭头。

Element is to relationship as object is to arrow.

此示例的简单性掩盖了从集合到类别的转变中的几个重要微妙之处，这些微妙之处将在此处和后续章节中详细阐述。首先，请注意小于符号“指向”较矮的人，而箭头指向较高的人。关系是有方向的。方向在语法上（图表上）由每个箭头的方向捕获，在语义上由对象和箭头之间的两个额外关系（映射）捕获，将在下一节（第 2.1.2 节）介绍。重要的是语法和语义之间的关系是一致的。例如，比较顺序关系Ann > Bob和箭头Ann ← Bob之间的对应关系。

The simplicity of this example belies several important subtleties with the move from sets to categories that are elaborated here and in subsequent sections. First, notice that the less-than symbol “points” to the shorter person whereas the arrow points to the taller person. Relationships are directed. Direction is captured syntactically (diagrammatically) by the direction of each arrow and semantically by two additional relations (maps) between objects and arrows, introduced in the next section (Section 2.1.2). What matters is that the relationship between syntax and semantics is consistent. Compare, for instance, the correspondence between order relationship Ann>Bob and arrow Ann←Bob.

其次，一般来说，序关系用“小于或等于”符号 ≤ 来表示，而不是用小于符号。因此，例如， Ann 比 Bob 矮或和Bob一样高，现在写为Ann ≤Bob ，意味着Ann 不比 Bob 高。这种解释的意义在于Ann 不比 Ann 高，即自导向关系Ann ≤Ann ，它对应于箭头Ann → Ann。严格小于的序关系，即a < b但不a ≤ b（因此a ≰ a），称为严格序。例如，parent-of就是严格序。这种区别与范畴论的相关性将在稍后阐述（第 2.1.3 节）。

Second, in general, order relationships are expressed using the “less-than or equals” symbol, ≤ , rather than the less-than symbol. So, for instance, Ann is shorter than or the same height as Bob, now written Ann ≤ Bob, means that Ann is not taller than Bob. The significance of this interpretation is that Ann is not taller than Ann, that is, the self-directed relationship Ann ≤ Ann which corresponds to the arrow Ann → Ann. Order relations that are strictly less-than, that is, a < b but not a ≤ b (whence, a ≰ a), are called strict orders. For example, parent-of is a strict order. The relevance of this distinction to category theory will be elaborated shortly (Section 2.1.3).

第三，实体之间一般可以存在多种关系，因此一对对象之间可以存在多个箭头。引用箭头和对象对的符号用于区分类别中的箭头。例如，假设Ann 比 Bob 年轻或与 Bob 同龄，则用顺序关系Ann ⊑ Bob来表示，即Ann不比 Bob 年长。Ann和Bob之间的两种顺序关系对应的箭头的完整引用形式为 ≤ AB : Ann → Bob和 ⊑ AB : Ann → Bob，即使用不同的符号或标识符来区分同一对对象之间的不同箭头。

Third, there may be more than one relationship between entities, in general, hence more than one arrow between a pair of objects. Notation referencing the arrow and the pair of objects serves to distinguish arrows in a category. Suppose, for instance, Ann is younger than or the same age as Bob is expressed by the order relationship Ann ⊑ Bob, that is, Ann is not older than Bob. The arrows corresponding to the two order relationships between Ann and Bob are referenced in full as ≤AB:Ann → Bob and ⊑AB:Ann → Bob, that is, different symbols or identifiers are used to distinguish different arrows between the same pair of object.

第四，也是最后一点，范畴论通常提供多种方法来对感兴趣的领域进行建模。例如，集合A和B之间的（二元）关系R可以建模为与参与该关系的元素相对应的对象之间的箭头集合，如Ann-Bill-Cal示例所示：对于与元素b ∈ B存在R关系的每个元素a ∈ A ，都有一个箭头a → b。因此，这种箭头被称为关系，而不是关系。或者，正如我们稍后将看到的（第 2.1.5 节，示例 2），R可以建模为这些集合A → B之间的单个箭头。当关系和关系之间的区别不重要时，使用术语关系。

Fourth, and finally, category theory often affords more than one way to model a domain of interest. For instance, a (binary) relation R between sets A and B can be modeled as a collection of arrows between objects corresponding to the elements partaking in that relationship, as in the Ann-Bill-Cal example: there is an arrow a → b for each element a ∈ A that is in an R relationship with an element b ∈ B. For this reason, such arrows are referred to as relationships, rather than relations. Or, as we will see later (Section 2.1.5, example 2), R can be modeled as a single arrow between those sets, A → B. The term relation is used when a distinction between relation and relationship is not essential.

2.1.2. 关系的方向是域先于余域

2.1.2. Relations are directed as domains before codomains

如前所述，每个关系的方向由每个箭头的方向表示：例如，≤AB : Ann → Bob从对象Ann指向对象Bob，这表示Ann在Bob之前，而Bob在Ann之后。对象Ann和Bob分别称为箭头≤AB的定义域和余域。

As mentioned earlier, the direction of each relationship is depicted by the direction of each arrow: e.g., ≤AB:Ann → Bob is directed from object Ann to object Bob, which says that Ann comes before Bob and Bob comes after Ann. The objects Ann and Bob are called the domain and the codomain of the arrow ≤AB, respectively.

之前与之后的关系就如同域与余域的关系。

Before is to after as domain is to codomain.

表达式 ≤ AB : Ann → Bob和 ≤ AB : Bob ← Ann标识同一个箭头，其方向性由对象和箭头之间的两个映射决定。通常，对于类别C ， C对象的集合表示为C 0 ， C箭头的集合表示为C 1。两个从箭头到对象的映射dom : C 1 → C 0和cod : C 1 → C 0分别确定每个箭头的定义域和陪域对象。在Ann-Bob-Cal 的例子中，相应的类别表示为ABC，由对象集ABC 0 = { Ann, Bob, Cal }、箭头集ABC 1 = { ≤ AB , ≤ BC , …  } 以及包含dom : ≤ AB ↦ Ann和cod : ≤ AB ↦ Bob的映射组成。

Expressions ≤AB:Ann → Bob and ≤AB:Bob←Ann identify the same arrow, whose directionality is determined by two maps between objects and arrows. In general, for a category C, the collection of C-objects is denoted C0 and the collection of C-arrows is denoted C1. Two maps from arrows to objects dom:C1 → C0 and cod:C1 → C0 determine the domain and codomain object of each arrow, respectively. In the Ann-Bob-Cal example, the corresponding category, denoted ABC, consists of the set of objects ABC0 = {Ann, Bob, Cal}, the set of arrows ABC1 = { ≤AB, ≤BC, … } and mappings that include dom: ≤AB ↦ Ann and cod: ≤AB ↦ Bob.

请注意，除了对象和箭头之间的关系之外，没有提到它们的性质。如何解释这些形式概念取决于上下文，即它们所在的类别。例如，在集合和函数的上下文中，对象是集合，箭头是函数。范畴论可以看作集合论构造的抽象；因此，符号和命名法通常取自那里：例如，箭头（态射、映射）通常写为f : A → B，即使对象不必是集合，箭头不必是函数（同态，以保留其关系的方式映射元素）。

Notice that nothing is said about the nature of object and arrow beyond their relationship to each other. How one is supposed to interpret these formal concepts depends on context, that is, the category in which they reside. For instance, in the context of sets and functions, an object is a set and an arrow is a function. Category theory can been seen as an abstraction of set theoretical constructions; hence, the notation and nomenclature are often taken from there: e.g., arrows (morphisms, maps) are generally written f:A → B even though objects need not be sets and arrows need not be functions (homomorphisms, mapping elements in a way that preserves their relationships).

2.1.3. 自导关系作为身份箭头

2.1.3. Self-directed relations as identity arrows

如前所述，顺序关系通常用 ≤ 符号表示。因此，A ≤ B表示A不晚于B。对于Ann-Bob-Cal 的例子，这种情况意味着每个人都是相对于自己排序的：例如，Ann ≤ Ann表示Ann不比自己高。因此，对应类别ABC的箭头集包括自导向箭头Ann → Ann、Bob → Bob和Cal → Cal 。如果每个元素a ∈ A都与自身相关，则A上的关系R被称为自反的（具有反身性） 。顺序的自反性对应于某个作为类别的有序集合中每个对象A的箭头 ≤ A : A → A。箭头 ≤ A : A → A称为A处的恒等箭头。

As also mentioned earlier, order relationships are typically expressed using the ≤ symbol. Thus, A ≤ B says that A comes no later than B. For the Ann-Bob-Cal example, this situation means that each person is ordered with respect to themselves: e.g., Ann ≤ Ann means that Ann is not taller than herself. Hence, the set of arrows for the corresponding category, ABC, includes the self-directed arrows Ann → Ann, Bob → Bob and Cal → Cal. A relation R on A is called reflexive (has the reflexivity property) if every element a ∈ A is related to itself. Reflexivity of order corresponds to an arrow ≤A:A → A for each object A in some ordered set as a category. The arrow ≤A:A → A is called the identity arrow at A.

关系之于反身性，就如同箭头之于身份。

Relationship is to reflexivity as arrow is to identity.

类别中的每个对象A都与一个恒等箭头相关联，写为 1 A : A → A。例如，在集合和函数的语境中，集合A处的恒等箭头是恒等函数 1 A : a ↦ a 。将恒等箭头表示为 1 A的原因类似于将数乘以 1（第 2.1.4 节）。自导箭头不一定是恒等箭头。例如，集合A上将每个元素a ∈ A发送到同一个元素k ∈ A的常数函数，即f : A → A ; a ↦ k，是自导的但不是恒等函数。两个兼容箭头的组合是一个箭头（第 2.1.4 节）。组合规则意味着类别中的每个对象都与一个且仅一个恒等箭头相关联。因此，对于类别C ，对象和箭头之间存在另一种关系，由映射id : C 0 → C 1给出；答↦1答。

Every object A in a category is associated with an identity arrow, written 1A:A → A. For example, in the context of sets and functions, the identity arrow at set A is the identity function 1A:a ↦ a. The reason for denoting the identity arrow as 1A is by analogy to multiplication of a number by 1 (Section 2.1.4). A self-directed arrow need not be an identity arrow. For instance, the constant function on a set A sending every element a ∈ A to the same element k ∈ A, that is, f:A → A; a ↦ k, is self-directed but not an identity function. The composition of two compatible arrows is an arrow (Section 2.1.4). The rules for composition imply that every object in a category is associated with one and only one identity arrow. Thus, for a category C, there is another relation between objects and arrows that is given by the map id:C0 → C1; A ↦ 1A.

在实体没有自我导向关系的情况下，每个对象都与一个身份箭头相关联，因此在类别的身份箭头方面缺乏有意义的解释，这种暗示对于认知科学来说似乎过于严格。但是，如后面所解释的（第 2.1.6 节），这些情况可以用其他类别建模。

The implication that every object is associated with one identity arrow may seem too restrictive for cognitive science in situations where the entities do not have self-directed relationships and thus lack a meaningful interpretation in terms of the identity arrows of a category. However, as explained later (Section 2.1.6), these situations can be modeled by other categories.

2.1.4. 关系的传递性作为箭头的组合

2.1.4. Transitivity of relations as composition of arrows

顺序关系本身以一种称为传递性 的方式相互关联。例如，Ann 比 Bob 矮，而Bob 比 Cal 矮意味着Ann 比 Cal 矮。所有有序元素三元组都以这种方式关联，即顺序关系的传递性。形式上，两个集合A和B之间的关系R是所有元素对集合的子集，其中每对的第一个元素来自A，每对的第二个元素来自B，称为A和B的笛卡尔积，即集合R ⊆ A × B = {( a, b )| a ∈ A, b ∈ B }。如果对 ( a, b ) 在R中，则我们说a与b是R 关联的，或写为aRb来表示这种关系。如果A中所有元素为 a, a ′, a ″ 的三元组都有 aRa ″ ，那么A上的关系R （即A × A的子集）就称为传递性（具有传递性）。顺序传递性对应于箭头的合取：如果存在箭头A → B和箭头B → C，那么就存在复合箭头A → C。这种箭头合取是组合的一种形式。

Order relationships are themselves related to each other in a way that is called transitivity. For instance, Ann is shorter than Bob and Bob is shorter than Cal implies Ann is shorter than Cal. All triples of ordered elements are related this way, that is, the transitivity property of order relations. Formally, a relation R between two sets A and B is a subset of the set of all pairs of elements with the first element of each pair drawn from A and the second element of each pair drawn from B, called the Cartesian product of A and B, that is, the set R⊆A×B = {(a, b)|a ∈ A, b ∈ B}. If the pair (a, b) is in R, then we say that a is R-related to b, or write aRb to indicated this relationship. A relation R on A, that is, a subset of A×A, is called transitive (has the transitivity property) if aRa′ and a′Ra″ implies aRa″ for all triples of elements a, a′, a″ in A. Transitivity of order corresponds to conjunction of arrows: If there is an arrow A → B and an arrow B → C, then there is a composite arrow A → C. This conjunction of arrows is a form of composition.

关系（顺序）与传递性的关系，就如同箭头与组合（连接）的关系。

Relationship (order) is to transitivity as arrow is to composition (conjunction).

在任意范畴C中，若f : A → B和g : B → C是一对相容箭头，即第一个箭头f的余域是第二个箭头g的定义域，则在C中存在一个从A到C的复合箭头，写为g ◦ f : A → C，其中 ◦ 表示组合运算，简称为组合。组合是一个（部分定义的）映射，将每对相容箭头送往它们的组合，即映射comp : C 1 × C 1 → C 1 ; ( f, g ) ↦ g ◦ f，也表示为 ◦ (−, −)，参见表达式 +(1, 2) 和 1+2。对于集合和函数，相容函数f : A → B和g : B → C的组合是复合函数g ◦ f : A → C；a ↦ g ( f ( a )) 将每个元素a ∈ A映射到元素g ( f ( a )) ∈ C，读作a 的 f 的 g，因此记为序。请注意，在这个集合和函数的上下文（类别）中，组合是将 f 和 g 发送给该类别中的某个函数 h 的函数，即g ◦ f = h，参见1 + 2 = 3。序箭头 ≤ AB : A → B和 ≤ BC : B → C 的组合的对应形式为 ≤ AC = ≤ BC ◦ ≤ AB : A → C，其中组合起着连接的作用。在这个上下文中（有序集作为类别），组合是序关系的函数。这种情况与传递推理的替换逻辑规则为aRb ∧ bRc ⇒ aRc，其中 ∧ 为合取。

In any category C, if f:A → B and g:B → C are a pair of compatible arrows, that is, the codomain of the first arrow, f, is the domain of the second arrow, g, then there is a composite arrow from A to C in C, written g ◦ f:A → C, where ◦ denotes the composition operation, simply called composition. Composition is a (partially defined) map sending each pair of compatible arrows to their composition, that is, the map comp:C1×C1 → C1; (f, g) ↦ g ◦ f, also denoted ◦ (−, −), cf. expressions +(1, 2) and 1+2. For sets and functions, composition of compatible functions f:A → B and g:B → C is the composite function g ◦ f:A → C; a ↦ g(f(a)) mapping each element a ∈ A to the element g(f(a)) ∈ C, read g of f of a, hence the notational order. Note that in this context (category) of sets and functions, composition is a function on functions sending f and g to some function h in that category, that is, g ◦ f = h, cf. 1+2 = 3. The corresponding form for composition of order arrows ≤AB:A → B and ≤BC:B → C is ≤AC = ≤BC ◦ ≤AB:A → C, where composition takes on the role of conjunction. In this context (ordered set as a category), composition is a function on order relationships. This situation compares with transitive inference as the logical rule of replacement aRb∧bRc⇒aRc, where ∧ is conjunction.

序和函数的组合满足两个重要的性质，这两个性质一般是任何范畴中箭头集合的组合运算所要求的：结合性（2.1.4 节）和单位性（2.1.4 节）。

2.1.6. 结构作为对象、结构关系作为箭头的类别

2.1.6. Categories of structures as objects and structural relations as arrows

到目前为止介绍的例子涉及的对象类别是元素或元素集，没有其他“内部”结构，也就是说，对象本身的组成部分之间没有关系。但是，实体可能具有需要建模的额外内部结构。例如，相似性判断可以被视为一组相关感知状态的映射，因此相似的刺激会引起相似的反应。解决这种情况的一种方法是按照域建模的方式对实体的内部结构进行建模，即将其作为现在属于“更大”类别的对象。然而，这种方法可能过于严格，因为一组单独不是类别的对象可能仍然构成一个类别。另一种方法是考虑一个较弱的结构概念，其中组成部分之间的关​​系不需要是反身的（不需要身份箭头）或传递的（不需要箭头的组合）。例如，parent-of既不是反身的，也不是传递的。对于这种情况，构造类别的一种有效方法是使用现有类别的内部结构，这可以避免证明新构造满足类别要求的需要。一个典型的例子是从集合和函数类别Set构造图和图同态类别（第 2.1.6 节）。从祖先类别（例如Set）构造类别也可用于模拟感兴趣领域之间的结构关系：如果构造的对象也是一个类别，则此类对象之间的映射对应于类别之间的映射。这种情况的一个典型例子是幺半群和幺半群同态类别（第 2.1.6 节）。当目标对象是一个一般类别时，这种方法会导致类别之间映射的定义，称为函子，它将在后面讨论（第 2.1.6 节）。

The examples introduced so far involved categories of objects that were elements or sets of elements with no other “internal” structure, that is, there were no relationships between the constituents of the objects themselves. However, an entity may have additional internal structure that requires modeling. For instance, similarity judgments may be regarded as a map on a set of related perceptual states so that similar stimuli evoke similar responses. One approach to this situation is to model the internal structure of an entity in the same way as domains are modeled, that is, as categories that are now objects in a “larger” category. Yet, this approach can be too restrictive as a collection of objects that are not categories individually may still constitute a category collectively. An alternative approach is to consider a weaker notion of structure, where the relations between constituents need not be reflexive (identity arrows are not required) or transitive (composition of arrows is not required). For instance, parent-of is neither reflexive, nor transitive. An efficient way of constructing categories for such situations is to use the internal structure of an existing category, which can circumvent the need to prove that the new construction satisfies the requirements of a category. A prototypical example is to construct the category of graphs and graph homomorphisms from the category of sets and functions, Set (Section 2.1.6). Constructing categories from a progenitor category, such as Set, can also be employed to model structural relations between domains of interest: If the constructed object is also a category, then maps between such objects correspond to maps between categories. A prototypical example of this situation is the category of monoids and monoids homomorphisms (Section 2.1.6). When the target object is a general category, this approach leads to a definition of map between category, called functor, which is taken up later (Section 2.1.6).

2.1.6.1. 内部结构

一种较弱的结构概念是有向图，以下简称图，它由一组顶点、一组顶点之间的有向边，以及确定每条边的源顶点和目标顶点的两个映射组成（定义 7）。由于图由集合和函数给出，因此可以根据集合的内部结构构造图。图不必每个顶点都有一个环（即，源和目标是同一个顶点的边），也不必每条（连通的）路径都有一条边。集合A上的关系R对应于一个图，其中每个元素a ∈ A都有一个顶点，每个关系aRa ′ 都有一条从a到a ′ 的边。因此，图对应于不需要自反或传递的关系。

A weaker notion of structure is directed graph, simply called graph hereafter, which consists of a set of vertices, a set of directed edges between vertices, and two maps determining the source and target vertex of each edge (Definition 7). Since a graph is given by sets and functions, graphs can be constructed from the internal structure of Set. A graph need not have a loop for each vertex (i.e., an edge whose source and target are the same vertex), or an edge for each (connected) path. A relation R on a set A corresponds to a graph with a vertex for each element a ∈ A and an edge from a to a′ for each relationship aRa′. Hence, graphs correspond to relations that need not be reflexive or transitive.

这种方法也适用于有序集。回想一下（第 2.1.3 节），严格有序集不是类别，因为它缺乏自反性，因此没有相应的恒等箭头。但是，严格有序集的集合与图一样，是一个类别。严格有序集是图的一种特例，它没有环或循环，即没有来自/到同一顶点v 的边或路径，对应于关系v ≤ v，并且从每个源到每个目标最多有一条边。对象是严格有序集，箭头是单调函数，即函数f : P → Q，使得对于所有元素p ∈ P ， p < p ′ 意味着f ( p ) < f ( p ′) 。作为比较，非严格有序集 ( P , ≤) 上的单调函数满足p ≤ p ′ 意味着对于所有p ∈ P ，f ( p ) ≤ f ( p ′) 。

This approach also applies to ordered sets. Recall (Section 2.1.3) that a strictly ordered set is not a category because it lacks reflexivity, hence the corresponding identity arrows. However, a collection of strictly ordered sets is a category in the same way as for graphs. A strictly ordered set is a special case of a graph that lacks loops or cycles, that is, edges or paths from/to the same vertex v corresponding to the relationship v ≤ v, and at most one edge from each source to each target. The objects are strictly ordered sets and the arrows are monotonic functions, that is, a function f:P → Q such that p < p′ implies f(p) < f(p′) for all elements p ∈ P. For comparison, a monotonic function on a non-strict ordered set, (P, ≤), satifies p ≤ p′ implies f(p) ≤ f(p′) for all p ∈ P.

2.2. 函子和表示

2.2. Functors and representation

与组合性一样，表征概念也是范畴论和认知科学的核心。认知表征通常被认为是一种心理状态，它与世界状态之间存在某种对应关系，而世界状态可以包括其他心理状态；组合（认知）表征则意味着组成心理状态之间的关系与世界状态之间的关系相对应。例如，假设世界状态中John 位于 Mary 的左边。将认知视为一种思维语言（Fodor，1975），假设至少有一个表示John的符号John，以及一个表示Mary的符号Mary，它们以这样的方式并列，即John和Mary之间的空间关系由它们对应符号之间的句法关系来表达：例如，符号对（John，Mary）。这些符号表征应该通过同样保留对应关系的物理实例映射来映射到相应的大脑状态（例如，参见Fodor and Pylyshyn，1988，脚注 9）。范畴是作为组合性的一种形式引入的。范畴之间的映射称为保留范畴结构的函子。函子提供了范畴论中关于组合认知表示和实例化的概念。

Like compositionality, some notion of representation is central to category theory and cognitive science. A cognitive representation is usually taken to mean a mental state that stands in some correspondence relation to a state of the world, which can include other mental states, and a compositional (cognitive) representation means that the relationships between constituent mental states correspond to relationships between the constituent states of the world. Suppose, for example, a state of the world that has John is to the left of Mary. Viewing cognition as a language of thought (Fodor, 1975), for instance, supposes at least a symbol for John, John, and a symbol for Mary, Mary, that are juxtaposed in such a way that the spatial relationship between John and Mary is expressed by the syntactic relationship between their corresponding symbols: e.g., the pair of symbols (John,Mary). These symbolic representations are supposed to map to corresponding brain states by a physical instantiation mapping that likewise preserves the corresponding relations (see, e.g., Fodor and Pylyshyn, 1988, footnote 9). Category was introduced as a form of compositionality. A map between categories is called a functor preserving categorical structure. Functors afford a category theory notion of compositional cognitive representation and instantiation.

现在，将范畴的定义转换为对象和箭头的集合，并将其结构关系转换为这些集合之间的映射，其价值显而易见。范畴之间的映射，即函子，直接就是保留这些结构关系的同态，由相等条件指定（定义 11）。

The value of casting the definition of category as collections of objects and arrows and their structural relations in terms of maps between those collections now becomes apparent. A map between categories, that is, a functor, is straightforwardly just a homomorphism preserving those structural relations, specified by equality conditions (Definition 11).

Definition 11 (functor). Suppose categories C and D. A functor F:C → D is a pair of maps (F0, F1):(C0, C1) → (D0, D1) that preserves

•域和余域：dom ( F 1 ( f )) = F 0 ( dom ( f )) 和 cod ( F 1 ( f )) = F 0 ( cod ( f ))，

• domains and codomains: dom(F1(f)) = F0(dom(f)) and cod(F1(f)) = F0(cod(f)),

•恒等式：F 1 (1 A ) = 1 F 1 ( A ) 且

• identities: F1(1A) = 1F1(A) and

•组成：F 1 ( g ◦ f ) = F 1 ( g ) ◦ F 1 ( f )

等式条件的等价交换图（在定义 11 中）清楚地表明，函子只是构成范畴结构的映射的（同态）态（注释 12）。

The equivalent commutative diagrams for the equality conditions (in definition 11) make plain that a functor is simply a (homo)morphism of the maps that constitute the structure of a category (remark 12).

注释 12函子的条件等价地由交换图给出。域和余域的条件由交换平方给出，其中dom1 = id ◦ dom且cod1 = id ◦ cod（图 7A ）。恒等（图 7B）和组合（图 7C ）的条件同样以此方式表示。因此，范畴是四个映射的总和，这四个映射指定：（1）组合运算，comp：C 1 × C 0 C 1 → C 1，（2）范畴中每个箭头的定义域为关联的恒等箭头，dom1：C 1 → C 1，（3）范畴中每个箭头的余域为关联的恒等箭头，cod1：C 1 → C 1，以及（4）与范畴中每个对象关联的恒等箭头，id：C 0 → C 1。这四个箭头之和就是箭头struct = comp + dom1 + cod1 + id，其中加法符号表示二元映射（参见示例 14），即struct : C • → C 1，其中C •表示C 1 × C 0 C 1 + C 1 + C 1 + C 0。（C 1 + C 1也写作 2 C 1。）类别C和D分别由箭头struct C和struct D给出，从struct C到struct D 的映射是交换平方（图 7D）。因此，函子是类别同态。

Remark 12. The conditions for a functor are equivalently given by commutative diagrams. The conditions for domains and codomains are given by commutative squares, where dom1 = id ◦ dom and cod1 = id ◦ cod (Figure 7A). The conditions for identity (Figure 7B), and composition (Figure 7C) are likewise expressed this way. So, a category is given as the sum of four maps that specify: (1) the composition operation, comp:C1×C0C1 → C1, (2) the domain of each arrow in the category as the associated identity arrow, dom1:C1 → C1, (3) the codomain of each arrow in the category as the associated identity arrow, cod1:C1 → C1, and (4) the identity arrow associated with each object in the category, id:C0 → C1. The sum of these four arrows is the arrow struct = comp+dom1+cod1+id, where the addition symbol expresses alternative maps (see Example 14), that is, struct:C• → C1, where C• denotes C1×C0C1+C1+C1+C0. (C1+C1 is also written 2C1.) Categories C and D are given by arrows structC and structD, respectively, and a map from structC to structD is a commutative square (Figure 7D). Hence, a functor is a category homomorphism.

由于函子可以用交换平方来表示，所以它们也可以以满足结合律和统一性条件的方式进行复合，也就是说，范畴、函子和函子复合构成了另一个范畴（注13）。

Since functors are expressed as commutative squares, they also compose in a way that satisfies associativity and unity conditions, that is, categories, functors, and functor composition constitute another category (Remark 13).

注释 13。交换平方复合为交换平方，因此函子F : C → D和G : D → E的复合为函子，G ◦ F : C → E。因此，（小）范畴、函子和函子复合构成另一个范畴Cat。（在此上下文中，小意味着对象和箭头的集合是集合，而不是真类。）

Remark 13. Commutative squares compose as commutative squares, hence composition of functors F:C → D and G:D → E is a functor, G ◦ F:C → E. So, (small) categories, functors, and functor composition constitute another category, Cat. (In this context, small means that the collections of objects and arrows are sets, not proper classes.)

组合表示的一种简单方法是取乘积。乘积由乘积函子构成（示例 14）。例如，假设 John 和 Mary 的符号集S = { John , Mary }，乘积函子构造符号对集S × S = {( John , John ), ( John , Mary ), …  }，可用于表示John 在 Mary 左边的情况。

A simple way of composing representations is to take products. Products are constructed from product functors (Example 14). For instance, assuming a set of symbols for John and Mary, S = {John,Mary}, the product functor constructs the set of symbol pairs S×S = {(John,John), (John,Mary), … }, which can be used for to represent the John is to the left of Mary situation.

例 14 (积/余积函子)。积函子和余积函子分别向它们的积函子和余积函子发送成对的对象和箭头，即 Π:( A, B ) ↦ A × B , ( f, g ) ↦ f × g和 ∐:( A, B ) ↦ A + B , ( f, g ) ↦ f + g。在集合与函数类别集合中，两个集合的乘积是（表示为）它们的笛卡尔积，两个函数f : A → C和g : B → D的乘积是乘积函数，f × g :( a, b ) ↦ ( f ( a ), g ( b ))。两个集合的余积是（表示为）它们的不相交并集，两个函数f和g的余积是余积函数，f + g : A + B → C； (1, a ) ↦ f ( a ), (2, b ) ↦ g ( b )。集合和函数的余积作用类似于交替：如果a是集合A中的一个元素，则应用函数f : A → C，否则应用函数 g : B → C。

Example 14 (product/coproduct functors). The product and coproduct functors send pairs of objects and arrows to their products and coproducts, respectively, that is, Π:(A, B) ↦ A×B, (f, g) ↦ f×g and ∐:(A, B) ↦ A+B, (f, g) ↦ f+g. In the category of sets and functions, Set, the product of two sets is (designated as) their Cartesian product and the product of two functions f:A → C and g:B → D is the product function, f×g:(a, b) ↦ (f(a), g(b)). The coproduct of two sets is (designated as) their disjoint union, and the coproduct of two functions f and g is the coproduct function, f+g:A+B → C; (1, a) ↦ f(a), (2, b) ↦ g(b). The coproduct for sets and functions acts like alternation: if a is an element from set A, then apply function f:A → C, otherwise apply function g:B → C.

积函子将类别C中的对象转化为C中的对象（积）。这样，可以重复构造以生成组合表示，其成分本身就是组合的，就像思维语言所假设的那样。例如，Sue 在 John 的左边，John 在 Mary 的左边，这可以用一个构造来表示，该构造涉及对 ( Sue , ( John , Mary ))，捕获层次关系，也就是说，Sue在John和Mary的左边。解决这种情况的一般方法涉及幺半群类别（Mac Lane，1998；Leinster，2014），即幺半群的泛化，其中集合被类别替换，二元运算被函子替换。在这种情况下，函子是一种张量积（例 15），它提供了一种范畴（符号-向量）形式的组合表示（Coecke 等人，2010）。采用张量积网络的联结主义（神经网络）模型（Smolensky, 1990）本质上涉及一类向量空间和线性函数的此类函子。

A product functor takes objects in a category C to (product) objects in C. In this way, constructions can be reiterated to generate compositional representations whose constituents are themselves compositional as is supposed for a language of thought. For instance, Sue is to the left of John who is to the left of Mary may be represented by a construction that involves the pair (Sue, (John,Mary)) capturing a hierarchical relationship, that is, Sue is to the left of both John and Mary. A general approach to this situation involves monoidal categories (Mac Lane, 1998; Leinster, 2014), that is, a generalization of monoid where the set is replaced with a category and the binary operation with a functor. In this context, the functor is a kind of tensor product (Example 15), which affords a categorical (symbolic-vectorial) form of compositional representations (Coecke et al., 2010). Connectionist (neural network) models employing tensor product networks (Smolensky, 1990) essentially involve such functors for a category of vectors spaces and linear functions.

例 15 (张量积)。张 量积 是形式为 F : C × C → C的函子，也写作⊗ (−, −) ，对一对对象( A, B )和一对箭头( f, g )的作用分别写作 A ⊗ B 和 f ⊗ g。结合性和单位性条件只需满足 自然同构（见定义 16），即A ⊗ ( B ⊗ C )≅( A ⊗ B ) ⊗ C 和 A ⊗ I ≅ A ≅ I ⊗ A，其中 I是 C中的特殊对象，其作用类似于幺半群的单位元素e的作用。例如，积与余积函子（例 14）是张量积：关于集合，现在将其看作幺半群范畴，笛卡尔积与不相交并集分别以任意单元素集合和空集作为单位对象。

Example 15 (tensor product). A tensor product is a functor of the form F:C×C → C, also written ⊗ (−, −) with the action on a pair of objects (A, B) and a pair of arrows (f, g) written A ⊗ B and f ⊗ g, respectively. Associativity and unity conditions need only hold up to natural isomorphism (see Definition 16), that is, A ⊗ (B ⊗ C)≅(A ⊗ B) ⊗ C and A ⊗ I≅A≅I ⊗ A, where I is a special object in C whose role is analogous to the role of the unit element, e, of a monoid. For instance, product and coproduct functors (Example 14) are tensor products: in regard to Set, now seen as a monoidal category, the Cartesian product and disjoint union with any one-element set and the empty set as the unit objects, respectively.

实体及其表征也自然地被视为驻留在不同的领域，因此涉及不同范畴之间的函子。例如，前面提到的将符号表达式映射到大脑状态的物理实例化概念，被比喻为将句法关系保留为大脑状态之间关系的函子，即满足等式F [ P&Q ] = B ( F [ P ], F [ Q ]) 的映射F，其中B是组合表达式P和Q 的相应大脑状态的函数（参见Fodor and Pylyshyn，1988，脚注 9）。此条件与函子的组合条件F 1 ( g ◦ C f ) = F 1 ( g ) ◦ D F 1 ( f ) 进行比较，其中 & 对应于领域范畴 C 中的组合运算◦ C，B对应于陪域范畴D中的组合运算 ◦ D。表达式P和Q以及它们对应的大脑状态F [ P ] 和F [ Q ] 是各自范畴中的箭头。或者，表达式和大脑状态可以被视为幺半群范畴中的对象，因此映射被视为幺半群函子（Mac Lane，1998），即保留幺半群范畴结构的函子。

Entities and their representations are also naturally regarded as residing in different domains and so involve functors between different categories. For instance, the aforementioned notion of a physical instantiation mapping of symbolic expressions to brain states is likened to a functor that preserves syntactic relations as relations between brain states, that is, a mapping F that satisfies the equality F[P&Q] = B(F[P], F[Q]), where B is a function combining the corresponding brain states for expressions P and Q (see Fodor and Pylyshyn, 1988, footnote 9). This condition compares with the composition condition for functors, F1(g ◦C f) = F1(g) ◦ D F1(f), where & corresponds to composition operation ◦ C in domain category C and B corresponds to composition operation ◦ D in codomain category D. The expressions P and Q and their corresponding brain states, F[P] and F[Q], are arrows in their respective categories. Alternatively, the expressions and brain states can be regarded as objects in a monoidal category, whence the mapping is seen as a monoidal functor (Mac Lane, 1998), that is, a functor preserving the structure of a monoidal category.

通过专门研究具有附加属性的函子，我们可以更多地谈论用于认知表征的函子方法。例如，伴随函子（Mac Lane，1998；Leinster，2014）是一个函子F：C → D，它带有一个对立函子G：D → C，后者充当伪逆，因为组合G ◦ F将C中的对象和箭头发送给C中与原始对象和箭头紧密相关但不一定相同的对象和箭头。（同样，组合F ◦ G将D中的对象和箭头发送给D中紧密相关的对象和箭头。）这种关系是一种自然变换，我们接下来将讨论它（第 2.3 节）。这种双向性形式可以应用于例如世界状态和大脑状态之间的往返关系（参见Ellerman，2016；Awodey 和 Heller，2020）。在认知表征和过程的背景下，这种伴随情况的另一个例子涉及预层（Mac Lane 和 Moerdijk，1992 ） ，它们是拓扑空间上的集值函子，作为类别，其中往返关系就像一个泛化过程，学习训练集可以扩展（泛化）到测试集上的正确响应（Phillips，2018，2020）。伴随情况涉及额外的类别理论概念，即通用构造（Mac Lane，1998；Leinster，2014），超出了本文介绍的详细比较范围（见第 3 节）。

Much more can be said about a functorial approach to cognitive representation by specializing to functors with additional properties. For instance, an adjoint functor (Mac Lane, 1998; Leinster, 2014) is a functor F:C → D that comes with an opposing functor G:D → C acting as a pseudo-inverse in the sense that the composition G ◦ F sends objects and arrows in C to objects and arrows in C that are closely related but not necessarily the same as the original objects and arrows. (Likewise, the composition F ◦ G sends objects and arrows in D to closely related objects and arrows in D.) That relationship is a natural transformation, which we turn to next (Section 2.3). This form of bidirectionality has applications, for example, in regard to the round-trip relationship between states of the world and brains states (see Ellerman, 2016; Awodey and Heller, 2020). Another example of this adjoint situation in the context of cognitive representations and processes involves presheaves (Mac Lane and Moerdijk, 1992) that are set-valued functors on topological spaces as categories, where the round-trip relationship acts like a generalization process in the sense that learning a training set extends (generalizes) to correct responses on a test set (Phillips, 2018, 2020). Adjoint situations involve additional category theory concepts, that is, universal constructions (Mac Lane, 1998; Leinster, 2014), that go beyond the detailed comparisons presented here (see Section 3).

2.3. 自然变换与比较

2.3. Natural transformations and comparison

认知过程通常被视为（认知）表征上的计算过程。自然变换是函子之间的映射，函子被解释为表征，因此自然变换可以解释为表征上的计算过程。然而，正如我们将在本节中看到的那样，自然变换也提供了与表征比较密切相关的解释。

Cognitive processes are generally regarded as computational processes over (cognitive) representations. Natural transformations are maps between functors, functors were interpreted as representations, so natural transformations can be interpreted as computational processes on representations. As we shall see in this section, however, natural transformations also afford a closely related interpretation as comparisons of representations.

Definition 16 (natural transformation, isomorphism). Suppose F, G:C → D are a pair of functors. A natural transformation is a family of D-arrows {ηA:F(A) → G(A)|A ∈ C0} such that G(f) ◦ ηA = ηB ◦ F(f) for all arrows f:A → B in C, which can be expressed as a commutative square (Figure 2). The examples of progressive matrices involve geometrical shapes, which highlight another aspect of category theory—generality. Up to this point, the examples have been about orders or simple algebraic structures. However, categories also exist for other kinds of structures including topological, metrical, and geometrical spaces. For instance, the category Vec has vector spaces for objects and linear maps for arrows. Thus, we can think of the shapes in terms of vector spaces and the transformations as linear maps such as dilation and rotation. The correspondence to categories, functors, and natural transformations is analogous, that is, the columns are functors from some category into Vec with shapes corresponding to vectors and arrows to linear transformations; likewise for the components of a natural transformation.

3.讨论

3. Discussion

范畴论对认知科学意味着什么？从广义上讲，范畴论（如认知科学）是关于通过保留该结构的映射来（重新）表示和比较组合结构。范畴论可能看起来像是令人眼花缭乱的抽象定义、例子和定理，但该理论的很大一部分围绕着一个简单的想法，即（类型化）交换平方，在各种背景和形式中，这与将认知视为（认知）表征上的计算过程系统的观点相一致，即某种版本的表征/计算心理理论（Wilson，1999）。

What is category theory to cognitive science? In a broad sense, category theory like cognitive science is about the (re-)representation and comparison of compositional structure via maps that preserve that structure. Category theory may appear as a bewildering array of abstract definitions, examples, and theorems, but a substantial amount of this theory organizes around a simple idea, that is, the (typed) commutative square, in various contexts and forms, that comports with the view of cognition as a system of computational processes over (cognitive) representations, that is, some version of a representational/computational theory of mind (Wilson, 1999).

表征（心理）状态应该通过结构对应关系捕捉世界的结构或其他心理状态，也就是说，所表示领域中实体之间的关系应该以某种一致的方式映射到表征领域中实体之间的关系（参见弗雷格的组合性原则或根特纳的结构映射理论）。交换平方以几何代数形式体现了这一思想，也就是说，“垂直”箭头是一个领域中的结构关系，它通过保持结构一致性的“水平”箭头传输或转换为垂直箭头作为另一个（可能相同）领域中的结构关系 —— 对某些（重新）表示的动作本质上与动作的（重新）表示相同。成为范畴、函子或自然变换的条件意味着并非任何类型化实体的平方都构成交换平方。范畴论以一种在形式框架中独一无二的方式对这个简单思想进行了广泛的形式概括。

Representational (mental) states are supposed to capture the structure of the world, or other mental states by structural correspondence, that is, the relations between entities in the domain being represented are supposed to map in some consistent way to relations between entities in the domain of representations (cf. Frege's compositionality principle, or Gentner's structure mapping theory). A commutative square embodies this idea in geometric-algebraic form, that is, a “vertical” arrow is a structural relation in one domain that is transported, or transformed to a vertical arrow as a structural relation in another (possibly identical) domain by “horizontal” arrows that maintain structural consistency—the action on some (re-)representation is essentially the same as a (re-)representation of an action. The conditions for being a category, functor, or natural transformation mean that not any square of typed entities constitutes a commutative square. Category theory provides a vast formal generalization of this simple idea in a way that is unique among formal frameworks.

范畴论在组合性概念方面与其他框架不同。组合性的经典形式依赖于标记概念，即，只要实体的表示被标记，实体成分的表示也会被标记（实例化、激活或铭刻）（Fodor 和 Pylyshyn，1988 年）。例如，红色圆圈的经典表示涉及红色和圆圈成分的相应表示的标记。组合操作不需要以这种方式标记箭头，如在幺半群的情况下所示，其中箭头 1 和 2 的组合是它们的加法 3，因为组合是一个将一对箭头发送到一个箭头的函数。标记的范畴论类似物是字符集合（字母表）上的自由幺半群，它由所有零个或多个字符的字符串的集合组成，即幺半群，其中·是连接，ϵ是空（长度为零）字符串：例如，字符串（箭头）“a”和“b”的组合是字符串“ba”（Walters，1991）。

Category theory differs from other frameworks in regard to the notion of compositionality. The classical form of compositionality turns on the notion of tokening, that is, the representations of an entity's constituents are tokened (instantiated, activated, or inscribed) whenever the entity's representation is tokened (Fodor and Pylyshyn, 1988). For instance, a classical representation of red circle involves the tokening of corresponding representations for constituents red and circle. A composition operation need not token arrows in this way, as illustrated in the case of monoids where the composition of arrows 1 and 2 is their addition 3, because composition is a function that sends a pair of arrows to an arrow. A category theory analog of tokening is the free monoid on a set of characters (alphabet) which consists of the set of all strings of zero or more characters, , that is, the monoid , where · is concatenation and ϵ is the empty (length zero) string: e.g., composition of strings (arrows) “a” and “b” is the string “ba” (Walters, 1991).

其他（相关）组合性概念以对象和箭头的乘积和余积的形式被提及：例如，将乘积函子应用于两个对象A和B，构造出乘积对象A × B。这究竟意味着什么，取决于所涉及的范畴和对象的具体选择。例如，在集合中，集合A和B的乘积被指定为它们的笛卡尔积，也写为A × B，还有两个函数可以检索每对（a，b）的组成部分，即a和b。一对对象可能有多个乘积，例如，B × A也构成A和B的乘积。这些例子似乎表明，这种形式的范畴组合性是经典组合性的一种版本，因为组成对象A和B被“标记”为复合对象A × B。但是，也存在其他集合的乘积，它们不涉及构造组成元素对。在有序集作为范畴的背景下，这种差异更加明显，其中两个对象A和B的乘积是最小值，即小于A和B 的最大对象。例如，在作为除数的范畴的有序集（例如，2 → 6 表示二是六的除数）中，12 和 9 的范畴乘积是最大公约数，即 3。范畴论对这种组合形式提供了精确定义和高度概括的概念，其中涉及极限和全称构造的概念（Mac Lane，1998；Leinster，2014）。极限是一种“最优”构造，即在给定范畴内可以做到的最好结果，也是表达该范畴内所有实例共同属性的全称构造（有关认知范畴内的介绍，请参阅Phillips，2021a ）。虽然这里没有讨论，但系统性（Fodor and Pylyshyn, 1988），即认知能力的共存，被视为（范畴的）普遍建构的结果（Phillips and Wilson, 2010）。

Other (related) notions of compositionality were alluded to in the form of products and coproducts of objects and arrows: e.g., the product functor applied to two objects A and B constructs the product object A×B. What that means precisely depends on the categories involved and the specific choice of object. For instance, in Set, a product of sets and A and B is designated as their Cartesian product, also written A×B, and two functions affording the retrieval of the constituents of each pair (a, b), that is, a and b. There may be more than one product for a pair of objects, for example, B×A also constitutes a product for A and B. These examples seem to suggest that this form of categorical compositionality is a version of classical compositionality in that the constituent objects A and B are “tokened” with the composite object A×B. However, other products of sets exist that do not involve constructing pairs of constituent elements. This difference is starker in the context of ordered sets as categories where the product of two objects A and B is the infimum, that is, the greatest object less than both A and B. For example, in an ordered set as a category of divisors (e.g., 2 → 6 says two is a divisor of six), the categorical product of 12 and 9 is the greatest common divisor, that is, 3. Category theory provides a precisely defined and vastly generalized notion of this form of composition that involves the concepts of limit and universal construction (Mac Lane, 1998; Leinster, 2014). A limit is a kind of “optimal” construction, that is, the best one can do in the given context and also a universal construction expressing a property common to all instances in that context (see Phillips, 2021a, for an introduction in the context of cognition). Though not taken up here, systematicity (Fodor and Pylyshyn, 1988), that is, the co-existence of cognitive abilities, is seen as a consequence of a (categorical) universal construction (Phillips and Wilson, 2010).

范畴论概念对认知科学的意义远不止于此。范畴、函子和自然变换的基本概念构成了范畴论的起点，就像组合、表示和计算（比较）的概念构成了认知科学的起点一样。自然变换可以提供推理，表示比较也可以。感知的类比可以说明这一点。通过比较双目视觉获得的图像，可以推断出到某个物体的距离。范畴论的一个类比是Tannakian 重建定理，它可以根据对象的表示类别重建该对象（NLAB，2010）。该定理的基础是Yoneda 引理（Mac Lane，1998；Leinster，2014），它是范畴论的一个基本结果（Riehl，2016），将对象的结构与其传入/传出箭头联系起来。重建定理被应用于关系模式归纳范式（Halford 等人，1998a）以解释学习迁移（Phillips，2021b）。重建涉及一种更高维形式的比较，称为二元函子之间的二自然变换，二元函子是两个类别的函子（参见双变量函数）。推理由模式（更一般地是代数）与其表示之间的对偶性（两种对立的关系）提供，类似于几何中众所周知的对偶性：两点确定一条线；对偶地，两条（相交）线确定一个点。为了进行比较，源和检测确定视线；对偶地，相交的视线确定源。重建涉及计算（双）函子的端点（ Mac Lane，1998），即“最佳”（通用）高维比较。

The import of category theory concepts to cognitive science does not end there. Base concepts of category, functor, and natural transformation constitute the starting point for category theory like the concepts of composition, representation and computation (comparison) constitute a starting point for a science of cognition. Natural transformations afford inference as do comparisons of representations. An analogy to perception provides an illustration. Inferring distance to an object is afforded by comparison of images obtained from binocular vision. A category theory analog is the Tannakian reconstruction theorem affording reconstruction of an object from its category of representations (NLAB, 2010). Underpinning this theorem is the Yoneda lemma (Mac Lane, 1998; Leinster, 2014), a fundamental result in category theory (Riehl, 2016) that relates the structure of an object to its afferent/efferent arrows. The reconstruction theorem was applied to the relational schema induction paradigm (Halford et al., 1998a) to account for learning transfer (Phillips, 2021b). Reconstruction involves a higher dimensional form of comparison, called a dinatural transformation between bifunctors, which are functors on two categories (cf. bivariate function). Inference is afforded by a duality—two opposing relations—between schemas (more generally algebras) and their representations that is analogous to a well-known duality in geometry: Two points determine a line; dually, two (intersecting) lines determine a point. For comparison, source and detection determine line of sight; dually, intersecting lines of sight determine the source. Reconstruction involves computing the end of a (bi)functor (Mac Lane, 1998), that is, the “best” (universal) higher dimensional comparison.

更高维度的构造与更高的认知能力相关，正如矩阵推理示例的比较中所提到的那样。当刺激沿着更多维度变化时，矩阵推理通常更困难（Carpenter 等人，1990 年；Kroger 等人，2002 年），这对应于认知复杂性的概念，即任务变化的维数（Halford 等人，1998b 年），这已被解释为范畴积（Phillips 等人，2009 年）。请注意，范畴、函子和自然变换也有相应的几何解释，即点、线和片，因此分别是零维、一维和二维对象。事实上，这些概念在高级范畴理论中统一为n 个单元的实例：例如，2-范畴的 (小) 类别为 0 个单元 (对象)，函子为 1 个单元 (0 个单元之间的箭头)，自然变换为 2 个单元 (1 个单元之间的箭头)。这种维度概念类似于函数或关系的顺序— — 二阶关系是 (一阶) 关系之间的关系 — — 是认知复杂性的另一种度量 ( Zelazo 和 Frye，1998 年)。

Higher dimensional constructions relate to higher cognitive capacities, as alluded to in the comparison of matrix reasoning examples. Matrix reasoning is generally more difficult when the stimuli vary along more dimensions (Carpenter et al., 1990; Kroger et al., 2002) corresponding to a notion of cognitive complexity as the number of dimensions of task variation (Halford et al., 1998b), which has been interpreted in terms of categorical products (Phillips et al., 2009). Note that category, functor and natural transformation also have corresponding geometrical interpretations as points, lines, and sheets, hence as zero-dimensional, one-dimensional, and two-dimensional objects, respectively. Indeed, these concepts unify in higher category theory as instances of n cells: e.g., a 2-category that has (small) categories as 0 cells (objects), functors as 1 cells (arrows between 0 cells), and natural transformations as 2 cells (arrows between 1 cells). This notion of dimensionality is akin to the order of a function or relation—a second-order relation is a relation between (first-order) relations—as another measure of cognitive complexity (Zelazo and Frye, 1998).

范畴论对认知科学的重要性似乎被许多可以归为次要来源的技术细节所掩盖。然而，什么才是关注的重点取决于手头的任务。转向更高级的构造不仅仅是提供更一般的概括（抽象），而是调和看似对立的具体性需求。组合的结合性和统一性条件就是这种情况的例子，它们在初等代数中被视为对应项时似乎无害，但对范畴论和认知科学而言却至关重要。例如，结合性条件是从hom 函子（Mac Lane，1998；Leinster，2014）之间的自然变换中恢复的，它决定了感兴趣范畴中给定对象的传入/传出箭头。对于 hom 函子之间的自然变换，交换平方的顺时针和逆时针遍历对应于两种替代组合顺序，即h ◦ ( g ◦ f ) 与 ( h ◦ g ) ◦ f。认知对应物涉及双路线理论（Kahneman，2011；Evans and Stanovich，2013），这里简单地说明了计算 (13 × 47) × 0 与 13 × (47 × 0) 的相对难度，这提供了一种范畴论方式来思考和实证研究双路线认知过程（Phillips 等人，2016，2017） 。尽管表面上看是“什么都不做”的箭头，但统一条件的恒等箭头也具有根本的重要性。Tannakian 重建依赖于 Yoneda 引理，而 Yoneda 引理又依赖于具有恒等箭头。恒等箭头作为引理证明的 hom 箭头集中的元素出现（例如，参见Leinster, 2014），本质上奠定了抽象的基础，参见弗雷格的语境性原理，即一个词的意义由其他词的语境决定（Janssen, 2001）。例如，米田引理被视为一种通过主观经验与其他主观经验的关系来评估主观经验的方式（Tsuchiya and Saigo, 2021）。米田/Tannaka 的这种观点为范畴论作为自指比较对认知科学的意义提供了补充。

The import of category theory to cognitive science may seem obscured by the many technical details that could be relegated to a secondary source. However, what counts as the primary focus of attention depends on the task at hand. Moving to higher constructions is not about simply affording more general generalizations (abstractions), but rather reconciling a seemingly opposed need for concreteness. This situation is exemplified with the associativity and unity conditions for composition, which seem innocuous when viewed as counterparts in elementary algebra, but are of fundamental importance to category theory and by comparison cognitive science. For instance, the associativity condition is recovered from a natural transformation between hom-functors (Mac Lane, 1998; Leinster, 2014), which determine the afferent/efferent arrows for a given object in the category of interest. The clockwise and anticlockwise traversals of the commutative square for the natural transformation between hom-functors correspond to the two alternative orders of composition, that is, h ◦ (g ◦ f) vs. (h ◦ g) ◦ f. A cognitive counterpart concerns dual-route theories (Kahneman, 2011; Evans and Stanovich, 2013), simply illustrated here by the relative difficulty of calculating (13 × 47) × 0 vs. 13 × (47 × 0), which affords a category theory way of thinking about and empirically investigating dual-route cognitive processes (Phillips et al., 2016, 2017). The identity arrows for the unity condition are also of fundamental importance despite appearances as the “do nothing” arrows. Tannakian reconstruction depends on the Yoneda lemma which in turn depends on having identity arrows. The identity arrows, appearing as elements in a hom-set of arrows for the proof of the lemma (see, e.g., Leinster, 2014), essentially ground the abstraction, cf. Frege's principle of contextuality, whereby the meaning of a word is determined in the context of other words (Janssen, 2001). For instance, the Yoneda lemma is seen as a way of assessing a subjective experience by its relationships to other subjective experiences (Tsuchiya and Saigo, 2021). This Yoneda/Tannaka perspective suggests an addendum to the import of category theory to cognitive science as self-referential comparison.
